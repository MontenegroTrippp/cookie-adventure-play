The Great Cookie Adventure!

Scene 1: Hello, Friends!

(Lights up. Varan walks on stage, waves to the audience.)

Varan: Hello, little friends! 👋 Have you seen my best friends — Lemur and Lan?

(Waits for reaction. Kids shout: “No!”)

Varan: We came from faraway Africa — where there’s no Wi-Fi, but so many stars! ✨ We’re searching for small, round, super tasty things… What are they called?

(Kids: “Cookies!”)

Varan: Yes! Cookies! 🍪 We walked through jungles, deserts… But in the very end, I lost my friends… Want to know how it all began?

(Kids: “Yes!”)

Varan: Then… the adventure begins!

Scene 2: The Mysterious Discovery

(Sound: bird cries. Ostrich runs in, flapping wings, speaking “German” — funny sounds. Points to a box of cookies.)

Lemur: Whoa! What’s this? Rocks? Poison mushrooms?

Lan: Or… aliens?

Lemur (shouting to the sky): Friends from other planets! We are peaceful! Come out!

(Old Porcupine appears. Takes a cookie. Eats. Closes eyes.)

Old Porcupine: Oooo… Cookies…

Lemur (horrified): Elder! You ate a whole UFO! There were aliens inside!

Old Porcupine (calmly): No panic, kids. That’s not a spaceship. That’s a cookie. When I was tiny… they took me to the city. Kept me in a cage. But fed me well. (Sadly.) People called these round things cookies. I loved them so much… (Takes another, gives one to Lan.) Try it.

Lan (takes a bite): Mmm… Sweet!

Lemur (suspicious): What if it’s poison?

(Old Porcupine gives him one. Lemur bites. Eyes widen.)

Lemur: This… this is… THE BEST THING I’VE EVER TASTED!

(Animals grab cookies. Laugh. Jump. Celebrate.)

Old Porcupine (to himself): Ah… porcupine, porcupine… You told it all again… (Waves paw, walks off.)

Scene 3: Dance of the Dream

🎵 Song: “We Want Cookies!” 🎵

(Upbeat, dreamy music. Dance.)

— Lemur pretends to win a trophy.
— Lan tries “air cookies”.
— Varan gazes into the distance.
— All sing:

“Cookies, sweet and round and fun,
Through the jungle we will run!
If we’re together, we won’t fall —
We’ll find you all — and eat them all!”

Scene 4: The Plan and the Escape

(Night. Three friends whisper under a tree.)

Varan: We need a plan.

Lemur: Plan? I’ll just punch the danger!

(Punches air.)

Lan: But what if there are poachers? I’m scared…

Lemur: Scaredy-deer!

Lan (stands up): I’m not scared! I’m just… careful!

Varan: We go together.

(They place paws together.)

All: Jungle! River! Water! We are friends — we are family!

(Run off. Music: “Mission Begins!”)

Scene 5: Panic in the Village

(Animals run around, panicked.)

Animals: They’re gone! Where’s Lemur? Where’s Varan?

Old Porcupine: Hmm… I think they went to the city. For cookies.

(Dramatic music. All freeze.)

(Tiger slowly enters, proudly straightens shoulders.)

Tiger (grandly): I am the Prince of Cats! Jaguar! I will find them!

Animals: Oh, Tiger! Thank you! You’re our hero!

Tiger (proudly): No! No thanks! I do this… (pause, looks into distance) …for glory.

(Whispers to himself:)

Tiger: For glory…

(Exits. Animals bow.)

Scene 5.5: First Steps — The Dark Forest

(Dark. Light only on the three. Rustling, strange sounds. They walk in a line.)

  • A branch hits Lan on the nose — she screams.
  • Lemur jumps out, scares her — she falls.
  • Varan signals: “Shhh!” — a strange noise.
  • All freeze.
  • Out of the bushes — a hedgehog with a flashlight. Slowly waddles.

(They laugh. Lan holds her heart.)

  • Lemur: “It’s not a monster — just a hedgehog with a flashlight!”
  • Varan: “That means we’re on the right path!”

(They continue. Light slowly brightens.)

Scene 6: The Giant Beetle

(Friends walk. See Beetle building a bridge. They accidentally step on it — bridge collapses.)

Beetle (angrily): Hey! You ruined my bridge!

Varan: Sorry!

Beetle: Let’s compete — who can build a better one?

(Music: “Build It Up!”. Construction dance.)

  • Friends “build” a bridge with imaginary planks.
  • Beetle builds perfectly.
  • But looks at their work and says:

Beetle: Wow! You really tried! Great job! (Laughs.) You’re true builders! (Gives them a paper with a tree drawing.) Here — your “ticket”. It will help.

(Friends cheer, run off.)

Scene 6.5: On the Edge of the Desert

(Bright light. Heat. Friends wipe sweat. Lan is weak.)

  • Lemur points far: “There’s the desert!”
  • Varan takes out the “ticket” from Beetle — looks at it.
  • Lan falls on sand: “I can’t go on…”
  • Varan lifts her: “Hold on! We’re almost there!”
  • Lemur finds an old binocular — looks: “Something’s shining! The city?”
  • All look in one direction.
  • Music grows tense.
  • They decide: “Forward!”

Scene 7: Desert and Turtle

(Friends crawl from heat.)

Lan: I’m so thirsty…

Lemur: I can’t go on…

(Turtle appears. Very slowly.)

Turtle: I… remember… there… was… a well…

Lan: Where?!

Turtle: Well… I… forgot…

(Everyone shocked.)

Varan: But you said you remember!

Turtle: I remember there was one… but not where.

Lemur: I have an idea! (Pulls out two metal sticks.) Magic sticks! They feel water! (Makes a dowsing rod, walks. Sticks twitch.) Here! Dig here!

(They dig — water appears!)

All: Water! 🎉

(Dance: “Splash Dance” — kids clap and jump.)

Scene 8: Cheetah the Champion

(Friends walk, full of energy. Cheetah enters, posing in front of a “mirror”.)

Cheetah: I am the fastest! I am the champion!

Lemur: Race?

Cheetah (laughs): You? Against me? Ha!

Varan: Yes! But only if you run to that tree and back!

(Points far.)

Cheetah: Easy!

(Cheetah runs. Friends shout: “Faster! Faster!” — he speeds up, runs off stage.)

Varan: He got so excited, he outran himself!

(All laugh. They leave.)

Scene 9: Poachers and the Tribe

🎵 “We are strong, we are cool,
We’ll catch you — rule by rule!
Fish, forest, animals — ours,
We don’t care for nature’s powers!” 🎵

(Friends hide. Think.)

Lan: How do we scare them?

Lemur: I have a super idea! (They make masks from leaves and sticks.)

All (together): We are the Spirits of the Jungle! We will take you!

(They dance. Poachers scream:)

Poachers: Aaa! The Jungle Tribe! Run!

(They flee. Everyone laughs.)

Scene 10: Monkey the Dancer

(Monkey enters.)

Monkey: I saw your dance! But the best dancers are us — the monkeys! To go further — you must outdance me!

Lan: I’ll try!

(Dance battle. Lan dances funny. Monkey laughs.)

Monkey: Nice! You may pass!

(Leaves. Friends cheer, continue.)

Scene 11: Varan is Lost

(Dark. Thunder. Friends call out.)

Lemur: Varan! Where are you?

Lan: We must find him!

(Dance: “Search in the Dark” — slow, with flashlights.)

Scene 12: “That’s How It All Began…”

(Dark. One spotlight on Varan. He slowly walks in. Soft, thoughtful music.)

Varan (quietly, warmly): You know… That’s how it all began. I was walking… walking… And suddenly — thunder! Lightning! (Thunder. Flash of light.) (Pause.) And then… I woke up here… With you.

(Looks at the audience. Smiles.)

Varan: My friends… Lemur and Lan… I lost them. Maybe… you can help me? Let’s all call together! Maybe they’ll hear us?

Varan: Ready?

Audience (together): Yes!

Varan: Then let’s call…

All (together): Lemuuuur! Laaaan!

(Silence. No one appears.)

Varan (sighs): Doesn’t work… (Thinks.) But in Africa… we call differently. When we call friends — we call from the heart. All together: “MOAA-U-AAA!” (Loud, full of soul.)

All (together): MOAA-U-AAA!

(Thunder. Bright light. Flash. Music bursts in!)

(Lemur and Lan run from backstage! They hug.)

Lemur: Varan! We heard you!

Lan: That was the cry of friendship!

Varan (smiling): So… we found each other after all.

(They look at each other. Pause.)

Lemur: We didn’t find cookies…

Lan: But still…

Varan: …we walked the whole path.

Lemur: Through forests, deserts, bridges, races…

Lan: We scared the poachers!

Varan: And became a real family.

All (together): So… the most important thing isn’t cookies. It’s the adventure!

Scene 13: The End — Thank You, Friends!

(Warm, joyful music. All together.)

Varan: Thank you, friends! You helped us every step of the way!

Lemur: You shouted, danced, believed in us!

Lan: And because of you — we’re together again!

Varan: We didn’t find cookies… But we have amazing stories, real friendship, and the coolest adventure ever!

🎵 Final Song: “We Are Family!” 🎵

(All dance. Invite kids on stage. Throw paper cookies into the audience.)

“Jungle, river, night and day,
We will walk the long, long way.
Hand in hand, we’re never apart —
We are friends — we are family, heart to heart!”

Varan (into the mic, warmly): Thank you for being with us! Until the next adventure, friends!

All (together): Bye-bye! 👋

(Curtain. Applause. Bright music.)
